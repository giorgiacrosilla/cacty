Documentation

The project is intended to present a magazine about the Secret Organizations, divided in three issues and enriched by metadata.

The roles inside the group have been divided as follows:

  • Giorgia Crosilla tagged the texts related to the KKK and developed the 90s and Dystopian Future styles;
  • Salvatore Di Marzo worked on the texts on Skull and Bones and developed the Aldine and De Stijl styles;
  • Nicole Liggeri worked on the Freemasonry issue and developed the 1860 and 70s styles.
